The Platform

The Operating System for Modern Food Distribution

SUPPLYAI brings intelligence to the workflows that drive food distribution. Our platform is designed to reduce manual friction, improve execution, and create clearer visibility across demand, inventory, compliance, and financial operations.

01

Order Intelligence

Capture and structure demand from multiple formats, including voice, messaging, handwritten notes, and informal ordering patterns.

02

Forecasting & Procurement

Translate downstream signals into smarter purchasing decisions with better visibility into demand, seasonality, and replenishment timing.

03

Wholesale Automation

Support distributor operations through invoice automation, exception management, accounts receivable workflows, and process standardization.

04

Warehouse Intelligence

Improve warehouse efficiency and reduce working capital pressure through better slotting, restocking, and inventory planning.

05

Compliance Layer

Integrate documentation tracking and operational compliance into daily decision-making to reduce risk and improve audit preparedness.

The Result

A more connected supply chain—one that is easier to operate, easier to scale, and better equipped for long-term modernization.
